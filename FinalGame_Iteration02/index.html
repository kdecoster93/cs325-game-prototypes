  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • Alex Jones vs The World utilizes an advanced moving system that allows the player to move in all 2-Dimensional X and Y directions. This includes the ability to walk, jump, and sprint quickly across the map. The game also has random sounds for every combat move and the pain recieved and inflicted by said moves. Alex Jones vs The World also includes a blood splatter system that spawns blood on enemy AI in different locations across their sprites body. All of this comes with additional tech that allows enemy AI to move towards the player and attack them in different and unique ways.   • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype?
    • 1. I learned how to give elements within groups (enemies) the ability to launch projectile that can damage the player. 2. I learned how to create a health bar for my boss enemies and apply it to group members. 3. I learned how to debug Phaser 3 using the debug boolean in main. 4. I learned how to make a seamless transistion between two different levels. 5. I learned how to create the illusion of the player falling contained within a single bounded screen. 6. I learned how to add introductory dialogue to my game.
    What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
    • The hardest part in the process was giving different enemies within a group the ability to launch projectiles. Since I have to iterate through each enemy within a group to give them their behavior, I have to use different methods to give them the same abilities that a single sprite element would have. Groups are required to make the code simpler and easier to track many enemies on screen. Also, working on flying enemy AI was also difficult and different from ground based AI.

  • Prototype Goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating?
    • The main game mechanics of Alex Jones vs The World are the fighting mechanics, platforming mechanics, and enemy AI. All three of these mechanics are important to the overall gameplay experience as either of the three can dull the enjoyment a player receives if one is subpar. The fighting mechanics will consist of close combat and projectile moves. The platforming mechanics will include 2D movement at various speeds and jumping. The enemy AI should challenge the player to master these controls, but not be so overbearing that it appears unfair.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
    • Players should feel as if the controls are intuitive and natural and that the fighting mechanics are visceral and provide immediate audio and visual feedback upon damage being dealt or taken. Each enemy AI should serve a different role and individually be easy to deal with, but in large groups should require careful thought. Ultimately, the player should see the game as being a fair and challenging 2D fighting platformer that rewards and punishes the player for making the right and wrong decisions respectively. Additionally, the player should be amused and or laughing throughout their journey as they hear Alex Jones make ludicrous statements in response to the events occurring within the game.

    • The gameplay consists of 3 major gameplay mechanics. The first mechanic is focused on movement which allows the player to move in any two-dimensional direction via walking, running, and jumping. The movement mechanic is critical to master in order to progress in the game when moving from one platform to another. The second mechanic is combat which gives the player the ability to do damage to enemies via punches and kicks while also including projectile based moves that allow the player to attack enemies from a distance with a finite amount of shots. The last gameplay mechanic is the enemy AI. Each different enemy that the player encounters will serve a different role in engaging the player and will have a different type of weakness. Together, different groups of enemies can pose a challenge to the player where one by themselves may not. All three of these gameplay mechanics will work in unison to drive the player forward into an engaging experience.
