Astroblast!

  • Originality: What is original about your game?
    • Astroblast is a top down shooter style game that boasts realistic space flight as a game mechanic. Every movement made in space must be accompanied by an equal and opposite reaction in order to regain composure. Fight a teleporting alien enemy to increase your score and try to master the art of space combat!
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • Astroblast utilizes top down shooter technology that allows a player to aim and shoot in a 360 degree range around them. The game utilizes the same shooting mechanics for its titular alien enemy that relentless attempts to destroy the player. The most important technology however is focused on fine tuning the movement controls to react appropriately to the players in game velocity and acceleration.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype?
    • 1. I learned how to properly assign movement controls to movement actions on screen 2. I learned how to create a "game over" event. 3. I learned how to properly use text, audio, sprites, and sprite animation. 4. I learned how to setup an environment for a game and set the locations of in game assets.
    What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
    • The hardest part in the process of making this game was trying to figure out how to properly make realistic space flight controls. Unlike a lot of other games that only require you to set the velocity upon a keydown and upon a keyup set it back to 0, Astroblast requires me to program with acceleration and drifting. I had to play around with a lot of movement programming to reach an acceptable state of feeling.

    • How to play: When you start the game you will use the WASD keys to move your spaceship around the level. To your left there will be an enemy alien ship firing laser beams at you. You only have three health bars that do not regenerate so if the alien hits you three times then the game will end. You the movement keys to dart around the alien ship and left click the mouse on or towards the alien's direction to damage him. Upon doing enough damage, your score will increase and the alien will be forced to teleport to a random location of his choice to begin shooting at you again. Survive for as long as possible to increase your score.
    Controls: Movement Keys: WASD Laser Fire: Left Mouse click
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
