Meme Royale

  • Originality: What is original about your game?
    • Launch is a velocity focused game where the player must hold shift down in order to accelerate their spacecraft fast enough to break into the atmosphere. However, the player will also have to contend with enemies that attempt to block their exit. The player only has three lives so they must strive to avoid the attacks made against them all while trying to reach the max velocity! The original feature of this top down scrolling game and what seperates it from others like it is the transfer of the maps speed from the computer's control to the player. This creates the illusion that the player is accelerating their ship past the world below them.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
    • Launch utilizes map moving technology that creates an effect that makes the player believe they're accelerating the longer they hold the shift button down when in reality the map beneath them is moving faster. This game also utilizes enemy technology that pressures the player to move from one spot by shooting damaging laser at them. These enemies also are a part of a fine tuned spawning system that ensures they will continue to harass the player when they go too far off screen.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype?
    • 1. I learned how to incorporate multiple scenes into my game. 2. I learned how to build a background map using the Tiled map generator. 3. I learned how to create classes and incorporate them into a main game play scene. 4. I learned how to move a looping tile map that creates the illusion of movement.
    What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
    • The hardest part in the process of making this game was trying to make different classes and scenes work together and function properly. Many features that would have been in the game had to be put on the back burner due to bugs related to the shooting code not interfacing well with any variables or functions outside its scene. Another part that took a lot of time was designing the tiled map for the background of the game.

  • Prototype Goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating?
    • The game mechanic this prototype is evaluating is the illusionary effects of accelerating map speed in the background to make the player in the foreground seem faster. Another game mechanic that is being tested is how the enemies create a sense of spectacle and chase that create obstacles for the player.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
    • Players should feel as if they are barely avoiding the enemies pursuing them in their rear view mirror. They will constantly hold the shift button down moving faster and faster all the while getting ever closer to the finish line that is their max velocity.

    • How to play: Upon starting the game you can use the shift key to start accelerating your speed and will the WASD keys to move your spaceship in four directions. Enemies will appear behind the player as they move forward across the map and harass the player in an attempt to remove all three of their lives and restart the game. Hold down shift and avoid the bullets to win.
